This guide gets you connected to Autheo Testnet and holding your first THEO tokens in under 5 minutes.

## What you need

- A web browser with [MetaMask installed](https://metamask.io/)
- 2 minutes

## Step 1: Add Autheo Testnet to MetaMask

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the network selector">
    Click the network name in the upper-left corner of MetaMask (shows "Ethereum Mainnet" by default).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a custom network">
    Scroll to the bottom of the network list and click **Add a custom network**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter network details">
    Fill in the following:

    | Field | Value |
    |---|---|
    | Network name | Autheo Testnet |
    | Default RPC URL | `https://rpc1.autheo.com` |
    | Chain ID | `785` |
    | Currency symbol | `THEO` |
    | Block explorer URL | `https://evm-explorer.autheo.com/` |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and switch">
    Click **Save**. MetaMask will switch to the Autheo Testnet automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Get testnet THEO

1. Copy your wallet address from the top of MetaMask (the `0x...` value).
2. Open the [Autheo Testnet Faucet](https://testnet-faucet.autheo.com/).
3. Paste your address and request tokens.

The faucet distributes up to **2 THEO** per request, with a limit of two requests per 24-hour period.

## Step 3: Verify your balance

After a few seconds (target block time is ~5 seconds), your balance should update in MetaMask. You can also verify on the [block explorer](https://evm-explorer.autheo.com/) by searching your `0x...` address.
