The Referrals tab (`/referrals`) allows you to generate a personal referral code and earn commissions whenever someone uses your code to purchase a node license.

## How it works

- Buyers who use your code receive a **discount** on their purchase
- You (the referrer) earn a **commission** on that transaction
- Both percentages are set transparently by the Autheo smart contract (`AutheoCentralStore`)

## Generating your referral code

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Referrals tab">
    Click the **Referrals Code** tab in the top navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate your code">
    Click **Generate Referral Code**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the transaction">
    Approve the on-chain transaction in your wallet. Gas fees apply. This registers your unique referral code on the `AutheoCentralStore` contract.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy and share your code">
    Once the transaction confirms, your unique code is displayed. Click **Copy** to copy it to your clipboard and share it with others.

    ![Referral code generated and displayed](/images/node-sale-referral-code.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Referral card

The Referrals card displays:
- Your unique referral code
- The **commission percentage** you earn on referred purchases
- The **discount percentage** buyers receive when using your code

## Viewing your earnings

For a per-currency breakdown of commissions earned, go to the **Orders** tab and select the **All Orders With My Referral** sub-tab. Commission summary tiles at the top show your total earnings in each currency (ETH, USDT, USDC, and via payment gateway).

See [Order history](/web-apps/node-sale/order-history) for full details on the Orders tab.

## Using a referral code when purchasing

When configuring a purchase, enter a referral code in the **Promo Code** field on the Purchase page and click **Apply**. The discount is applied to your order immediately, and the referrer receives their commission on-chain when the transaction confirms.
