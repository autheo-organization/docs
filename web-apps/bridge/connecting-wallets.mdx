The Autheo Bridge supports wallets across multiple blockchain ecosystems. You must connect a wallet before initiating any transfer.

## Connecting your wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Bridge">
    Navigate to the Autheo Bridge URL in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect Wallet">
    Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top-right corner of the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your wallet type">
    A wallet selection modal appears listing supported wallet protocols. Select your preferred wallet type based on the chain you are transferring from.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve the connection">
    Approve the connection request in your wallet's popup window. Once connected, your wallet address is displayed in the top-right corner.

    ![Bridge connect wallet — step 3](/images/bridge-connect-wallet-3.png)

    ![Bridge connect wallet — step 4, wallet connected](/images/bridge-connect-wallet-4.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Your wallet must be on a network compatible with the selected source chain. If you are transferring from Arbitrum Sepolia, ensure your wallet is connected to Arbitrum Sepolia before initiating a transfer.
</Tip>

## Supported wallet types

| Ecosystem | Supported wallets |
|---|---|
| EVM (Ethereum, Arbitrum, Autheo Alpha) | MetaMask, WalletConnect-compatible wallets |
| Solana | Phantom |
| Cosmos | Keplr |
| Starknet | Supported via wallet selector |
| Radix | Supported via wallet selector |

For most users bridging between Arbitrum Sepolia and Autheo Alpha, **MetaMask** is the recommended wallet.

## Managing connected wallets

The **History sidebar** (accessible via the clock icon in the floating action buttons) shows your connected wallets alongside your transfer history. From this sidebar you can:

- View which wallet addresses are currently connected
- Review transfers associated with each connected address

To switch wallets, disconnect your current wallet from within the wallet extension and reconnect using the **Connect Wallet** button. To disconnect entirely, use your wallet extension's disconnect or revoke connection option.
