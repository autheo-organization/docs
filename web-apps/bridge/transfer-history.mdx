The Transfer History sidebar provides a chronological log of all cross-chain transfers made from your connected wallet.

## Accessing transfer history

Click the **clock icon** in the floating action buttons on the right side of the Bridge interface. The sidebar slides open alongside the main transfer form.

## What transfer history shows

Each entry in the history log includes the transfer details from your session and connected wallets. You can use transfer history to:

- Verify that a past transfer completed successfully
- Find the source-chain transaction hash for a specific transfer
- Track in-progress transfers that are waiting for Hyperlane relayer confirmation

For completed transfers, the status shows **Sent** once tokens have arrived on the destination chain.

<Note>
For in-depth transfer tracking across the Hyperlane network, you can also use the [Hyperlane Explorer](https://explorer.hyperlane.xyz) with your transaction hash.
</Note>
